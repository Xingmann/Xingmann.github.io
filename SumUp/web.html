PATTERN

PATTERN is a website that allows you to discover and build pattern through your interactions with the page. The page is designed to work on devices.
(Don’t forget to unlock your screen before playing!)

1. Tilt the device to rotate the shapes.
(every tap on the circle changes its color)
2. When the shapes match, indication shows up.


3. Rotate the device to change into different shapes!


4. shake the phone to go back to the default shapes.

BEAUTY : A Poster Series

BEAUTY is a series of five video posters questioning what beauty is. Derived from our desire for beauty, this series argues when various trends and styles contract and conflict with each other, do we still have a clear definition for what beauty is?

UNIT00 : Everything Interfaces

UNIT00 depicts the story of a machine escapes from human’s control, and disappears in the space through the control room perspective with interfaces. Aesthetic choices inspired by anime Evangelion.

UNHUMAN : A Self Portrait

UNHUMAN is a self portrait project which illustrates some thoughts in my head.
